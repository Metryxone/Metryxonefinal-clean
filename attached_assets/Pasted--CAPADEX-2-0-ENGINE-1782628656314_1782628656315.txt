MISSION

Establish a single engineering standard for the entire CAPADEX platform.

This phase defines HOW engineering is performed.

It does NOT redesign business functionality.

It does NOT activate new capabilities.

It creates the engineering rules every future enhancement must follow.

1. Audit current engineering architecture.

2. Identify architectural inconsistencies.

3. Define engineering standards.

4. Remove ambiguity.

5. Protect existing architecture.

6. Reduce technical debt through standards.

7. Improve maintainability.

8. Improve scalability.

9. Improve readability.

10. Prepare repository for future enhancement.
