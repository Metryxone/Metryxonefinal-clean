**Platform ceiling = L3 Managed** — and that is the honest, correct ceiling: L4 needs runtime-adoption
evidence (no production traffic yet) and L5 needs autonomous unreviewed optimization (out of scope; human
approval is authoritative by design). L4/L5 are **WITHHELD, not failed**.

## Maturity by axis (NEVER composited)
- **Structural maturity: L3** — the architecture, controls, and breadth are genuinely Managed-level.
- **Validation maturity: L2** — controls exist but quality/accuracy/load are largely **unvalidated** (no
  traffic). This is the honest ceiling on confidence today.
- **Production-confidence: null** — withheld by design (no runtime + outcome evidence). Not 0 — *unmeasured*.

## Overall maturity verdict
**STRUCTURALLY MANAGED (L3), VALIDATION-PENDING.** CAPADEX is a broad, well-architected, honesty-engineered
L3 platform. It is **not** L4+ and should not be claimed as such — the enhancements in this assessment are
precisely the work that (a) hardens L3 quality and (b) *instruments the evidence* needed to legitimately
measure toward L4 during a pilot. **Maturity is raised by maturation, not redesign.**
