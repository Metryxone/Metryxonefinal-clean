   the empty Outcome/KPI traceability columns. (13/14/15)

   realized-outcome evidence — by design, not by failure.
6. **9 terminology/definition conflicts (3 HIGH) + 6 apparent duplicates** are resolved canonically (04/10 of
   0.1) and adopted here; all HIGH conflicts are documentation/naming — **no code rename in this phase.**
7. **Tier-0 launch gates remain** (independent of the blueprint): demo/super-admin lockout, security-scan
   triage, DPDP/minor-consent. These are launch-safety gates, not blueprint gaps.
