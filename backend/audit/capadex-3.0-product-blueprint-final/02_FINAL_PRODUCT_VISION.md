# 02 · Final Product Vision

**FROZEN.** Adopted verbatim from Phase 0.1 (02), validated against `replit.md`, `docs/CAPADEX.md`, the
Operating-Model vision validation, and the engineering constitutions. No new vision is introduced.

## CANONICAL VISION (single source — FROZEN)
> **CAPADEX is a progressive behavioral-and-competency intelligence product that helps a person understand
> where they are, why, and what to do next — by assessing concerns, behaviors and competencies, diagnosing
> honestly, recommending grounded next actions with AI assistance, and guiding them across a defined growth
> lifecycle — for individuals and the institutions/employers who support them.** It is delivered on the
> MetryxOne SaaS platform.

## Mission
Move every person from **curiosity → insight → growth → mastery** with honest, evidence-grounded intelligence —
and equip the people and institutions who support them with role-appropriate, privacy-safe views.

## Vision (long-term direction)
The trusted behavioral-and-competency intelligence layer for education and work — one that proves impact
through realized outcomes, not just reports. (Outcome-proof is the explicit Program 1–6 direction; see 13.)

## Business purpose
Convert assessment into **decided, grounded action** at individual and institutional scale, with accountable AI
and enterprise-grade governance, privacy, and entitlement.

## Customer promise
- **To the individual:** "Understand yourself honestly, and know your next best step."
- **To the institution/employer:** "See your cohort/talent truthfully (k-anonymous), and act on evidence."
- **To everyone:** "We never fabricate. We separate what we know (Coverage) from how sure we are (Confidence),
  and we never blend them into one misleading score."

## Enterprise value
Accountable-AI spine (observe → diagnose → recommend → explain → override → govern), formal versioned
ontologies, k-anonymity by default, RBAC + audit + consent, and a single canonical operating model.

## Canonical objectives (consistent set)
1. **Assess** honestly across concern / behavior / competency lenses.
2. **Diagnose** with evidence; separate Coverage from Confidence; never fabricate.
3. **Recommend** grounded next actions; **AI assists, humans decide.**
4. **Guide** across the lifecycle (Curiosity → Insight → Growth → Mastery).
5. **Serve the support ecosystem** (parents, mentors, institutes, employers) with role-appropriate views.
6. **Report** outcomes and progress with k-anonymity and privacy by default.

## Honest scope boundaries (what the vision is NOT)
- **NOT yet outcome-proven** — realized-outcome capture is forward work (13; GAP-O1).
- **NOT autonomous** — maturity self-capped at **Managed**; AI never decides unilaterally.
- **NOT clinical** — no psychologist/clinical lens (07; do not claim it until built).

## Naming hierarchy (resolves the only vision-level conflict)
**MetryxOne** = the SaaS platform/brand. **CAPADEX** = the flagship product. "Behavioral / career / competency
intelligence" = three **lenses** on the one product, not three products.

## Verdict
**ONE canonical, internally consistent vision. FROZEN.** The only reconciliation ever required was brand-vs-
product naming and lens-vs-product framing — both resolved. No statement is fabricated or contradictory.
