## Headline findings (honest)
1. **ONE coherent product exists.** Vision is consistent once **MetryxOne (brand) ⊃ CAPADEX (product)** and the
   three "intelligence" framings are read as lenses, not separate products. (02)
2. **The lifecycle is 4 coded stages, not 5.** `CAP_CUR → CAP_INS → CAP_GRW → CAP_MAS`. **"Clarity" is a display
   alias of Insight; "Awareness" is uncoded.** This was the central reconciliation. (04/05/10-C1/C2)
3. **The mature core is Assess → Diagnose → Recommend → Report** (domains D2–D4). It is genuinely strong and
   non-duplicative.
4. **One systemic root gap: the back half (re-measure → realized outcome → KPI) is missing/partial everywhere** —
   surfacing independently as missing Outcome domain (D13), absent Exit/Continuous assessments, thin journey
   tails, and empty Outcome/KPI traceability columns. (03/07/08/09/11)
5. **Maturity ceiling is Managed/L3** (Progression + Outcome lag at Guided/L2); Intelligent/Enterprise/
   World-Class **honestly withheld** for lack of realized-outcome evidence.
6. **9 terminology/definition conflicts (3 HIGH) and 6 apparent duplicates** catalogued; the HIGH ones are all
   documentation/naming fixes — **no code rename in this phase.** (10)
7. **Tier-0 launch-critical gates remain:** demo/super-admin lockout (E1), security-scan triage (E2), DPDP/
   minor-consent (E3). (11)
