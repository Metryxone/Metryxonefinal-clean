# 02 · Canonical Product Vision

**Phase:** Program 0 / Phase 0.1 — Product Blueprint Validation & Consolidation. **Mode:** read-only,
repository-first, enhancement-planning-only. **Date:** 2026-06-30. **Goal:** reconcile every product-vision
statement scattered across prior phases into **exactly ONE** canonical vision.

## Sources reconciled
| Source | Vision phrasing (paraphrased) |
|---|---|
| `docs/CAPADEX.md` | "Progressive behavioral intelligence system" that moves a person across a lifecycle. |
| `replit.md` | "MetryxOne — Behavioral Intelligence SaaS Platform." |
| Operating-Model cert `02_PRODUCT_VISION_VALIDATION.md` | Multi-persona career/competency/behavioral platform; assess → understand → recommend → grow. |
| Enhancement-assessment | Behavioral + competency assessment with AI guidance and reporting. |
| Engineering constitutions (MX-700/800) | Reuse-first intelligence platform; honesty-engineered; Managed ceiling. |

## Conflict found
- **Naming duality:** product is "MetryxOne" (platform brand) and "CAPADEX" (the assessment/lifecycle
  product). These are **not in conflict** once stated as **brand (MetryxOne) ⊃ flagship product (CAPADEX)**.
- **Scope drift:** some docs frame CAPADEX as "behavioral intelligence," others as "career intelligence,"
  others as "competency assessment." These are three *lenses on one product*, not three products.

## CANONICAL VISION (single source)
> **CAPADEX is a progressive behavioral-and-competency intelligence product that helps a person understand
> where they are, why, and what to do next — by assessing concerns, behaviors and competencies, diagnosing
> honestly, recommending grounded next actions with AI assistance, and guiding them across a defined growth
> lifecycle — for individuals and the institutions/employers who support them.** It is delivered on the
> MetryxOne SaaS platform.

## Canonical objectives (consistent set)
1. **Assess** a person honestly across concern / behavior / competency lenses.
2. **Diagnose** with evidence, separating Coverage from Confidence; never fabricate.
3. **Recommend** grounded next actions; AI assists, **humans decide**.
4. **Guide** the person across the lifecycle (Curiosity → Insight → Growth → Mastery).
5. **Serve the support ecosystem** (parents, mentors, institutes, employers) with role-appropriate views.
6. **Report** outcomes and progress with k-anonymity and privacy by default.

## Honest scope boundaries (what the vision is NOT — carried from prior cert)
- NOT yet an *outcome-proven* product (realized-outcome capture is a gap — see 11).
- NOT *autonomous* — maturity is self-capped at **Managed**; AI never decides unilaterally.
- NOT a *clinical* product (no psychologist/clinical lens — see 06).

## Verdict
**ONE canonical vision exists and is internally consistent once brand vs product is separated.** The three
"intelligence" framings are lenses, not conflicting visions. No vision statement is fabricated or
contradictory; the only reconciliation needed was naming hierarchy (MetryxOne ⊃ CAPADEX) and lens-vs-product.
