# 12 · Canonical Product Blueprint (Draft)

**The single consolidated reference** the CAPADEX 99% Enterprise Launch Program will build against. Draft —
**human approval mandatory before it becomes canonical.** Composes deliverables 02–11; adds nothing new.

---

## 1. Product (from 02)
**CAPADEX** — a progressive behavioral-and-competency intelligence product on the **MetryxOne** platform.
Assess → Diagnose (honestly) → Recommend (AI-assisted, human-decided) → Guide across the lifecycle → serve the
support ecosystem → report with privacy. *Brand (MetryxOne) ⊃ product (CAPADEX); "behavioral / career /
competency intelligence" are three lenses on ONE product.*

## 2. Business domain model (from 03)
13 domains: **D1 Identity · D2 CAPADEX Assessment Core · D3 Competency · D4 Career · D5 Lifecycle/Progression ·
D6 AI Operating Model · D7 Recommendation/Intervention · D8 Reports/Analytics · D9 Employer/Hiring · D10
Education/Institution · D11 Commercial · D12 Platform Governance Intelligence · D13 Outcome & KPI.**
Core (D2–D4) mature; D5/D10/D11 partial; D12 dormant; **D13 missing-as-realized = keystone gap.**

## 3. Canonical lifecycle (from 05) — 4 coded stages
**Curiosity (CAP_CUR) → Insight (CAP_INS) → Growth (CAP_GRW) → Mastery (CAP_MAS).**
"Clarity" = display alias of Insight. "Awareness" = conceptual pre-stage, **uncoded**. Claim 4, not 5.

## 4. Canonical terminology (from 04) — top rules
Insight (not split into Clarity) · Concern≠Signal≠Behaviour≠Competency≠Construct · Outcome = realized+measured
only (≠Growth stage, ≠Development process) · Capability = product function (≠competency) · Mentor≡Coach
substrate · Stage (lifecycle) ≠ Phase (build) · Coverage⟂Confidence⟂Evidence · null≠0.

## 5. Persona architecture (from 06)
~9 first-class personas (students×3, fresher, professional, employee, HR, employer, institute). Partial:
faculty, teacher/counsellor, manager/leadership, L&D, parent-tail, mentor/coach-tail, NGO. Missing-dedicated:
government, healthcare, psychologist/clinical. **Two axes: segment coverage ⟂ journey completeness.**

## 6. Assessment taxonomy (from 07)
Strong core: Entry, Baseline, Diagnostic, Behaviour, Competency (+employer Performance). Partial: Progress,
Learning, Performance(learner). **Missing: Exit, Continuous.** LBI⟂Competency intentionally separate.

## 7. Journey model (from 08)
One lifecycle spine per persona: **Entry → Diagnose → Recommend → Act/Grow → (Re-measure) → Mastery/Outcome.**
Employer/institute journeys complete; learner journeys front-half complete; universal missing **outcome tail**;
two dead-ends (teacher/counsellor survey, parent/mentor tails).

## 8. Traceability (from 09)
Core capabilities trace across Domain/Persona/Stage/Journey/Assessment/AI/Report. **Outcome + KPI columns are
the dominant ✗** — same close-the-loop root.

## 9. Conflicts resolved (from 10)
9 conflicts (3 HIGH: Insight/Clarity, 5-vs-4 stages, Outcome conflation) — all documentation/naming, fixable
without code change. 6 apparent duplicates: 1 true (Mentor/Coach), 1 near (Teacher/Counsellor), 4 not-duplicates
(LBI⟂Competency, multi-entry CareerBuilder, `-v2` review-candidates, concern/behaviour overlap).

## 10. Gaps (from 11)
Tier-0 launch-critical: **GAP-E1 demo lockout · E2 security triage · E3 DPDP/minor-consent.** Tier-1
close-the-loop: **GAP-O1 outcome · K1 KPI · A4 exit/continuous · P1 progress-rerun · P2 mastery-gating.**
Plus persona/journey, terminology, and activation/maturity tiers.

## 11. Maturity & verdict (carried)
**Structural CERTIFIED · maturity Managed/L3 (ceiling) · CONDITIONAL GO for controlled pilot** after Tier-0
gates clear. Intelligent/Enterprise/World-Class **honestly withheld** (no realized-outcome evidence).

## 12. Single highest-leverage program theme
> **Close the loop.** Instrument the shared **re-measure → realized-outcome → KPI** tail ONCE; doing so
> simultaneously completes D13, the learner journey tails, the Exit/Continuous assessment types, the Outcome/KPI
> traceability columns, and lifts the maturity ceiling. This is the spine of the 99% Enterprise Launch Program.
