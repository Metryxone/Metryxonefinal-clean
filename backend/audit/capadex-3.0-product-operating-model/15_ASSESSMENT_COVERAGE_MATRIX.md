## Coverage findings (honest)
- **The coverage hole is unambiguous and systemic: EXIT and CONTINUOUS assessment are MISSING.** Every stage
  has entry/diagnostic; no stage has a mandatory exit re-test or an ongoing continuous-assessment cadence.
  This is the assessment-side expression of GAP-P1 (no measured growth, no closed loop).
- **Leadership and wellness are partial** — served by reports/screening, not by dedicated assessments.
- **Breadth is otherwise strong** — 8 of 9 domains have real coverage.

## Verdict
**Coverage: BROAD on domains, INCOMPLETE on purpose-types.** The missing exit + continuous assessments are the
top assessment enhancement (and the mechanism that would make progression measurable). Enhancement-only —
reuses existing engines re-administered at exit/interval.
