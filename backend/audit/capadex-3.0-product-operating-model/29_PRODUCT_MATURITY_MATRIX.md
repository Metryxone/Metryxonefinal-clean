## Maturity findings (honest)
- **Platform maturity ceiling = MANAGED (L3).** Most dimensions reach Managed; **Evidence** alone touches
  Intelligent/Enterprise (it is the strongest framework).
- **Two dimensions sit *below* Managed: Progression and Outcome/KPI** (Guided) — they are the platform's
  maturity-limiting factors and the root of nearly every gap in this audit.
- **Intelligent / Enterprise / World-Class are WITHHELD** — withheld because: AI accuracy unvalidated (no
  Intelligent), realized outcomes uncaptured + E1–E3 + dormant governance (no Enterprise), and no self-
  optimization by design (no World-Class). These are honest withholds, not failures.

## Verdict
**Overall product maturity: MANAGED (L3), with two Guided-level drags (progression, outcome/KPI).** To reach
**Intelligent**: AI quality harness + realized-outcome capture. To reach **Enterprise**: close E1–E3, activate
governance, measure scalability. World-Class is intentionally out of scope (governance self-cap).
