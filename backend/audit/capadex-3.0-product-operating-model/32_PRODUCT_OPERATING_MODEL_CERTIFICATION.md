# 32 · Product Operating Model Certification

Formal certification of CAPADEX as a Product Operating Model, on four **independent, never-composited** axes
(per the honesty contract: Coverage ⟂ Confidence ⟂ Evidence; Structural ⟂ Activation ⟂ Adoption ⟂ Outcome).

## The four certification axes
| Axis | Question | Verdict | Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Structural** | Is the product *built*? | **CERTIFIED** | 587 services, 134 canonical tables, all 12 domains + 8 report classes + 10 AI functions present (16,17,20,23). |
| **Integration / Coherence** | Do the pieces *connect* into one model? | **CERTIFIED (with seams)** | Traceability intact module→reports (17); seams: BE/FE stage naming, `-v2` duplication, outcome/KPI tail break. |
| **Activation** | Is it *turned on* end-to-end? | **PARTIAL** | 158/190 flags OFF; governance/RBAC v2 dormant; Connected≠Orchestrated (16,28). |
| **Production Confidence (Outcome)** | Does it *demonstrably work in production with realized outcomes*? | **WITHHELD (null)** | No realized-outcome data (GAP-O1); no load test (GAP-S1); E1–E3 open (27). null≠0. |

## Certification statement
> **CAPADEX is STRUCTURALLY CERTIFIED as a coherent, traceable Product Operating Model at maturity level
> MANAGED (L3).** Integration is certified with three named, non-redesign seams. **Activation is PARTIAL** (a
> large, honest, default-OFF dormant surface). **Production-Outcome confidence is WITHHELD** by design —
> realized-outcome evidence does not yet exist, and the platform correctly refuses to claim outcomes it has not
> observed.
>
> The labels **Intelligent**, **Enterprise-Ready**, and **World-Class are WITHHELD** — not because the product
> is deficient, but because the *evidence required to certify them does not yet exist* (AI accuracy harness,
> realized outcomes, scalability proof, governance activation). This is an honesty withhold, not a failure
> verdict.

## What would lift each withheld axis (traceable to Register 31)
- **Activation → CERTIFIED:** Tier-1.3 readiness guards + Tier-3.1 staged governance activation.
- **Production Confidence → MEASURABLE:** Tier-0 (E1–E3) + Tier-1.1/1.2 (close loop + realized-outcome capture)
  + Tier-2.7 (load test).
- **Intelligent maturity → CERTIFIABLE:** Tier-1.4 AI quality harness + Tier-1.2 outcome evidence.
- **Enterprise maturity → CERTIFIABLE:** Tier-0 + Tier-2.6 (commercial) + Tier-3.1 (governance) + Tier-2.7.

## Certification scope & integrity
- Read-only, repository-first; every verdict traces to a numbered deliverable (02–31) and measured evidence.
- No code/DB/business-logic was changed to produce this certification.
- No axis was composited into a single misleading score. **Structural-Certified is the honest headline; it is
  not "production-ready."**
