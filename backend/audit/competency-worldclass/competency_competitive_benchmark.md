## 2. Where MetryxOne Wins (real advantages)

1. **Behavioural/concern signal spine** — question→concern→construct linkage gives psychological granularity that pure skills-graph players (LinkedIn, Workday, Degreed) lack.
2. **Explainable, traceable recommendations** — full Concern→…→Intervention chain stored in `recommendation_explainability`. Most competitors' recs are opaquer.
3. **Honest provenance** — `curated` vs `onet_derived` badges; disallowed hiring verdicts. This *integrity posture* is a procurement asset vs black-box AI matchers.
4. **Integrated stack** — one engine spans student → professional → employer with a shared ontology (most rivals specialise in one segment).
5. **Modern science architecture** — IRT/EAP/Bayesian/4-fifths built in, ready to validate.

## 3. Where MetryxOne Loses (disadvantages)

1. **No validity evidence** — SHL/Korn Ferry/Mettl ship decades of reliability/validity/norms. MetryxOne has formulas, no studies.
2. **No norms / empty benchmarks** — competitors have population; MetryxOne is below k=30.
3. **Tiny live bank** — thousands of calibrated items vs ~50–100 static.
4. **No outcome/predictive evidence** — Eightfold/Korn Ferry cite outcome correlation; MetryxOne has zero realised outcomes.
5. **No scale / references / revenue** — competitors have enterprise install bases; MetryxOne is pre-revenue.
6. **Ontology not populated live** — O*NET not imported; 5 roles weighted vs full labour-market graphs (Workday/LinkedIn/Eightfold).

## 4. Missing Capabilities (to reach parity)

- Published technical/validity manual; norms by segment; large calibrated bank; DIF/bias reports on real data; outcome-correlation studies; ESCO/LinkedIn-graph interoperability; enterprise compliance (SOC2/ISO/DPA); scaled population.

## 5. Differentiators to Protect & Lead With

- **Explainable behavioural intelligence** (signal spine + traceable recs + honest provenance). If MetryxOne validates this with real outcomes, it has a **defensible, differentiated position** between rigid psychometric incumbents (SHL/Korn Ferry) and opaque AI matchers (Eightfold) — *transparent, behaviourally-grounded, outcome-validated competency intelligence.*

## 6. Honest Competitive Verdict

> MetryxOne is a **credible challenger by design** with one or two genuine differentiators, but it is **not yet competitive on the dimensions enterprise buyers weight most** — validated reliability/validity, norms, outcome evidence, scaled references, and revenue. Today it benchmarks as a **promising pre-validation entrant**, not a peer of SHL/Mettl/Korn Ferry.
