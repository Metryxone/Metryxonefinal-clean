1. **Future Skills = 0 competencies.** The classification revealed the genome has no AI/digital-era competencies yet ‚Äî a content gap to author, surfaced rather than hidden.

3. **Competency‚ÜíQuestion mappings = 0.** The single most valuable assessment link (which question measures which competency) is not yet authored. The machinery is ready for it.

None of these are broken engineering ‚Äî they are **authoring/content work** sitting on top of working engines.
