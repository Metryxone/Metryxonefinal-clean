## I.2 The smallest set of improvements to move EVERY layer above 90
Ordered; each item names the layers it lifts. The thesis: a handful of **back-half** additions
(outcomes + products + a single shared reference asset) lift most layers at once.

1. **Future Employability Index 2.0** *(compose-only, Low)* — reframe the live employability
   outcome/journey with a transferable/AI-resilient lens.
   → Future Readiness +, Product Activation +, Commercial +. **Highest leverage, do first.**
2. **AI-resilient skill taxonomy + occupation-exposure reference** *(Medium — the keystone)* — the
   one shared asset behind AI, Future-Skills, and Employability-2.0.
   → unlocks items 3–4; Future Readiness ++.
3. **`ai_readiness` outcome model + AI Career Navigator journey** *(Medium, additive — 7 outcome
   precedents)* → Future Readiness ++, Decision Intelligence +, Product Activation +.
4. **Future Skills Planner** *(Medium — extends WC-7B growth-plan bridge)* → completes the
   Decision→Growth→Mentor→**Skills** chain. Growth Plan Activation → 90+, Future Readiness ++.
5. **`career_resilience` outcome + Career Resilience Index** *(Low — composes the 395-concern
   adaptability corpus)* → Future Readiness +, Personalization → 90+, Longitudinal → 90+.
6. **Wire future L5B contexts → future outcomes + add Layoff/Obsolescence/Industry-Change context
   labels** *(Low-Medium)* → Decision Intelligence → 90+, Future Context relevance ++.
7. **Future-readiness SKUs on the existing CAP ladder** *(Low — WC-7C commercial precedent)* →
   Subscription Intelligence → 90+, Commercial Intelligence → 90+.
8. **(Defer) Entrepreneurship Index** *(High — needs content seeding)* — last; not required to clear
   90 on the other layers.

## I.3 Why this is the *smallest* set
Items 1–7 are dominated by **additive outcome models, one shared reference asset, and SKU wiring** —
all with existing precedents (7 outcome models, 6 journey routes, WC-7B bridges, WC-7C commercial).
No core-engine rewrite is required. Item 2 (the taxonomy) is the only genuinely new asset, and it is
the keystone three products depend on. Entrepreneurship (item 8) is the only content-seeding effort
and is explicitly deferred because no other layer's 90 depends on it.
