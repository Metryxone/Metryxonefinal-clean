## Measurement basis (all read-only, captured during this audit)
- Corpus sizes: **2,489** concern rows (`capadex_concerns_master`), **30,638** clarity questions
  (`capadex_clarity_questions`), each with an L5A stage row (`wc3_question_intelligence`) and an

- Outcome models: **7** (`wc3_outcome_models`) — 1 gated. Journey routes: **6**
  (`wc3_journey_routes`) — 1 stub (`corpus_pending`). Commercial ladder: CAP_INS / CAP_GRW /

## The one-line finding
> CAPADEX can already **ask and context-classify** future-readiness concerns (AI, transition,
> employability, entrepreneurship all exist as real L5B contexts), but the **activation back-half**
> — dedicated future-readiness *outcome models* and *products* — is missing for everything except
> Employability. Front-half present, back-half absent. That asymmetry is the whole of WC-8.
