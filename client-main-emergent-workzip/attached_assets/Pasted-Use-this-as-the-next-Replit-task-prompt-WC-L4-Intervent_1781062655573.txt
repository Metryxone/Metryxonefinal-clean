# Objective

Activate the first adaptive action layer of CAPADEX.

Convert existing intelligence into actionable interventions by reusing:

* Stage Intelligence
* Outcome Intelligence
* Journey Intelligence
* Decision Intelligence
* User Intelligence
* Behaviour Intelligence
* Trend Intelligence
* Forecast Intelligence

without introducing:

* new scoring models
* new constructs
* new ontology
* new AI engines

Interventions must be derived entirely from existing intelligence outputs.
