- **AI Career coverage:** ~0 — no occupation/role vulnerability mapping.
- **AI Outcome reachability:** **0%** — there is no `ai_readiness` outcome model and no AI journey

  generic `employability_index` / `career_builder`.

## B.3 The honest gap
CAPADEX has **AI *anxiety* intelligence** (it knows when a user is worried about AI) but no **AI
*adaptation* intelligence** (it cannot tell them what to do about it). The content front-half is
better than expected (440 contextually-tagged Qs); the missing pieces are: (a) an AI-resilient
skill taxonomy, (b) an occupation→exposure reference, (c) an `ai_readiness` outcome model, (d) an
AI Career Navigator product/journey.
